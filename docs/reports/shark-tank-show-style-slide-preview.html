The Guardrails

The agents can support write-capable paths, but humans stay in the approval loop.

No silent Studio changes

Studio configuration, visibility, permissions, knowledge sources, tools, and activation stay behind explicit human review.

No unattended Jira writes

Where approved write actions exist, the agent still pauses for human confirmation before creating, updating, transitioning, assigning, or commenting.

No unattended publishing

Confluence updates can be prepared through dry-run review, with explicit human approval before anything is finalized.

No launch authority transfer

Humans still own pilot approval, broad readiness, visibility, risky actions, production launch, and lifecycle decisions.
